Welcome to My Website!

Welcome! I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Golub Capital Social Impact Lab (Stanford GSB) and the Digital Economy Lab (Stanford HAI). I study ways to enhance political accountability in developing contexts, focusing on topics like the role of (mis)information, criminal-political violence, and policy coordination across government levels. My recent work leverages Generative AI to reduce misinformation sharing and improve online deliberation.

I received my Ph.D. in Political Economy and Government (PEG) from Harvard University in May 2023. My research has been published in the Journal of the European Economic Association and featured by Evidence in Governance and Politics (EGAP).
