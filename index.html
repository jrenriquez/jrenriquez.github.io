I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Golub Capital Social Impact Lab (Stanford Graduate School of Business), and at the Digital Economy Lab (Stanford Institute for Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence). I am also an Affiliated Researcher at the King Center on Global Development.

I study how to improve political accountability in developing contexts.
